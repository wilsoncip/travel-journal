Wilson's Travel Journal Project Proposal


This website will be a journal of all the places that I have traveled to in the last few years and will be formatted as a travel guide for viewers who are curious about traveling for their vacations. It will contain narratives about the places, along with pictures (most of which are self-taken), and various suggestions/advice throughout the journal. There will be multiple pages on this website, and each page will represent a different country. Each of these pages will contain all the information regarding my experience during my vacation, along with the different places I visited while in the country, and rank each place based on my overall enjoyment. These places will also be accompanied by various images and travel advice for the viewers.

The objective of this website is to help those who enjoy traveling for vacation by providing necessary info that comes from an objective perspective of another traveler, in this case, it would be me, by providing information that is relevant as a tourist. Furthermore, I can use this website to share my personal experience of the places that I have been, which viewers may read to help them decide on their vacation.

The target audience of this website is younger people (20-40 years) who are active and enjoy going to new places. Generally, it targets those who want to travel within the near future (0-2 years) and have a coming holiday or some free time to spare. This website will also target those who have the finances to afford a vacation outside of their country, and this may vary as this website will not target any specific region, hence prices may vary based on the distance of their travel. And as a niche, this website can also be targeted to those who do not travel as much or are not able to, allowing them to experience the travels through my perspective for their enjoyment.
